3d Printer Build Kickoff

March 1, 2024

After going down many rabbit holes surrounding the world of 3D printing, I have decided to build my own 3D printer.

There are many vibrant communities which focus on printer builds of varying complexity and cost such as 3D printers from the Voron Design, RepRap, and HevORT communities.

Having explored many of the options, I decided to build a Rook Mk2 printer from Rolohaun Design. This decision was based upon the relative ease to build this printer when compared to many of the other options. If all goes well, as a follow-on build, I would like to work on a Trident from Voron Design.

For the Rook MK2 printer, the main person and designer behind these is a person that goes by Rolohan, hence Rolohaun Design. He resides somewhere in Canada and he and the members of his 3D printer community are awesome and very helpful.

This is the kickoff of my blog updates to track the progress my progress in building the Rook Mk2 printer. It is primarily helpful to me and may be of benefit to others exploring the world of 3D printing.
